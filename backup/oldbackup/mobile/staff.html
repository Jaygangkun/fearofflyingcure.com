STAFF

THE CENTER FOR VIRTUAL REALITY THERAPY




Gerald Tarlow, Ph.D.
(Lic. # PSY5377)
Dr. Gerald Tarlow is co- director of the center. He has specialized in treating anxiety disorders for more than 30 years. He is an honorary Clinical Professor in the Department of Psychiatry at UCLA. Dr. Tarlow is board certified in cognitive and behavioral psychology by the American Board of Professional Psychology.




Alan Berkowitz, Ph.D
(Lic.# PSY 5957)
Dr. Berkowitz is co- director of the center. He received his Ph. D. in Clinical Psychology from The University of Colorado in 1977 and has been in private practice in the Los Angeles area since that time. Dr. Berkowitz is a member of the expert witness panel for the LA Superior Court. He is an Associate Fellow and clinical supervisor at the Albert Ellis Institute in New York, and a fellow of the Acadamy of Cognitive Therapy.



Nan Tarlow, Ph.D.
(Lic. #PSY11046)
Dr. Nan Tarlow has over 20 years experience in treating anxiety disorders. She has extensive experience in working with children, adolescents and adults.

Staff Of The Center For Virtual Reality Therapy

Gerald Tarlow, Ph.D.

Lic. # PSY5377

Dr. Gerald Tarlow is co- director of the center. He has specialized in treating anxiety disorders for more than 30 years. He is an honorary Clinical Professor in the Department of Psychiatry at UCLA. Dr. Tarlow is the only psychologist in Los Angeles who is board certified in behavioral psychology by the American Board of Professional Psychology.

Alan Berkowitz, Ph.D

Lic.# PSY 5957

Dr. Berkowitz is co- director of the center. He received his Ph. D. in Clinical Psychology from The University of Colorado in 1977 and has been in private practice in the Los Angeles area since that time. Dr. Berkowitz is a member of the expert witness panel for the LA Superior Court. He is an Associate Fellow and clinical supervisor at the Albert Ellis Institute in New York, and a fellow of the Acadamy of Cognitive Therapy.
